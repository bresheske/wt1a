Innovative Grant Award

Summer Grant Opportunity!

Greetings, fellow Title Educators! You are invited to apply for a special summer opportunity for the Wisconsin Title I Association's Innovative Grant Project. There is a one-month window to apply, with the application deadline due July 6, 2017 by 4PM. This grant will provide $2000 towards an innovative project that you would like to start in your school. We encourage you to apply!

Open application form

Contact Lisa Arneson with any questions - larneson@cesa3.org

2017 Grant Winners

  • Keshena Primary School - My Learning Box - Books and materials in a box that parents could use at home to support their child's learning.
  • Arcadia Elementary School - Give Me Five program - Materials are provided for support to parents for five minutes of math and literacy practice at home.

2016 Grant Winners
